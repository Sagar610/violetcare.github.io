Violet High Dependancy Care

Many assume that when the needs of an aging adult reach a certain point, their options for care are limited to a care facility.

Scroll

Specialized care for your loved one

It is easy to think that as we are getting older the options for care are limited to a care facility. Sometimes adult children can assume that despite what mum or dad wants they will have to go into a care home but this isn’t the case. There are means of keeping your parents at home with a skilled professional to take care of their needs on a one on one basis.

  • On call 24/7 for client needs
  • Flexible and fast scheduling
